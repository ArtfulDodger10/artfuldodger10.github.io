## Who I Am

I'm a **Malware Analyst** and **Reverse Engineer** who got into security the hard way — by breaking things, getting curious about how they broke, and going deeper until I understood why.

I started with CTFs, moved into reverse engineering, and now I spend my time dissecting real malware samples, understanding infection chains at the binary level, and building detections from observed behavior rather than assumptions.

This blog is where I document what I find — written the way I wish I'd found it when I was learning.
